Perview Portfolio

General Introdution:

This page includes several basic projects I did in the fall of 2020: project 1,2 and 3. I've called them the Human Universe Poem, the Memoir Video, and the Born and Death Game.

The Human Universe Poem:

I have always been curious about the universe, and also about the human body. The universe is as complex as the human body, and in many novels and movies, I think the universe and the human body run the same, so I wrote this poem.

The Memoir Video:

This is the video that my cousin and I shot when they went to Hawaii and other places in the past. We were happy and happy all the way. I edited them together in order to form a good time line and story for watching.

The Born and Death Game:

This is a little game I made online with Twine. The main purpose of this game is to show people that no matter what your birth and experience process is, the final result is the same. So we should recognize ourselves and the world, and keep a good attitude.
